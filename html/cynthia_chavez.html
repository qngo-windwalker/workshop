Cynthia Chavez

has been teaching, coaching, and mentoring in early childhood programs for the past 35 years. She is currently working with the Ysleta del Sur Pueblo as the Tuy Pathu Tigua Indian Lead Pre-K teacher. As a Reading Diagnostician, she has served as a Literacy Leader for the Ysleta Independent School District through the El Paso Collaborative for Academic Excellence housed at the University of Texas at El Paso.
