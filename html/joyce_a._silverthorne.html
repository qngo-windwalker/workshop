Joyce A. Silverthorne

,

Director of the Office of Indian Education, is an enrolled Salish member from the Confederated Salish and Kootenai Tribes. She has served as P-20 Policy Advisor in the Montana Office of Public Instruction, and on the Tribal Education Department National Assembly, Montana Board of Public Education, and National Advisory Council on Indian Education. Ms. Silverthorne earned an M. Ed. at the University of Montana at Missoula.
